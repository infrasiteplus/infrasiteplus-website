Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how InfraSitePlus ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The InfraSitePlus app may collect the following information to provide its services:

  • Employee Information: Name, attendance records, and work-related information.
  • Photos: Photos captured for reporting highway defects or site documentation.
  • Camera Access: Used for capturing defect images or viewing live camera feeds.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information to improve functionality and performance.

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing highway defect reports
  • Recording employee attendance
  • Providing live camera monitoring of infrastructure sites
  • Maintaining operational records and reports
  • Improving application performance and services

3. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Data may be stored securely on authorized servers or cloud infrastructure used by the organization operating the application.

4. Camera and Photo Permissions

The application requires access to the device camera to capture photos of highway defects and site conditions. These images are used strictly for operational and reporting purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to third parties. Data may only be shared with authorized personnel or organizations responsible for managing infrastructure operations.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill operational requirements and comply with applicable regulations.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for professional and organizational use and is not designed for individuals under the age of 13.
