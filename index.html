About Infra SitePlus

Built for the Complexity of Modern Infrastructure

Infra SitePlus is a digital command center designed for large-scale infrastructure projects. We help project teams monitor progress, manage compliance, and maintain operational control — from groundbreaking to final handover.

Born from real-world challenges on highways, bridges, and urban development sites, our platform brings together defects tracking, attendance, billing, claims, CCTV monitoring, and document management into one unified system.
